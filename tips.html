Tips

Add the following line to the bottom of the Sauce tab in 4chan-x. This will create an e621 link on posts with images that searches e621 for that image. It works like with other reverse image searches, but filters results to only those that are e621 posts (and NOT other e621 pages like search result pages).

https://www.google.com/searchbyimage?image_url=%IMG&safe=off&q=site%3Ae621.net/post/show/;text:e621
